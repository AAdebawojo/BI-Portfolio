Analysis on Student Mental Health

I utilised Tableau to analyse the relationship between mental health and academic performance among university students. The dataset, collected via a Google Forms survey, encompasses insights from 100 students regarding their mental well-being and its impact on their CGPA.

Key findings reveal that 35% of respondents experience depression, while 33% face panic attacks. The analysis also highlights that single females aged 18-19 in the Computer Science/IT Faculty are particularly affected, underscoring the need for targeted support initiatives. Additional insights include the distribution of students seeking faculty assistance for mental health issues based on gender and marital status, emphasising the critical nature of mental health resources in academic settings.
